I work at Google NetInfra. I completed my PhD at Department of Computer Science, Harvard University, working with Prof. Minlan Yu. Prior to 2018, I spent my two years of PhD at Yale University. I spent my first two years of PhD at NSL, Department of Computer Science, USC. I got my Bacherlor's degree from Tsinghua University. During my third and fourth undergrad years, I worked in Network Architecture & IPv6 Research Division with Prof. Jun Bi.
